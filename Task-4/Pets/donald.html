About Donald

Donald is a calm and gentle giant who loves lounging around and enjoying quiet moments. Despite his relaxed demeanor, he has a playful side and enjoys the occasional game of fetch. Donald is searching for a loving family who can provide him with the care and affection he deserves.

  • Breed: African Grey Macaw Parrot
  • Age: 2 years
  • Gender: Male
  • Personality: Gentle, Calm, Loyal
  • Vaccinated: Yes
  • Neutered: Yes
